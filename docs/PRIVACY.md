# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 1, 2026

---

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

---

## Data We Collect

### 1. Parent Information

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Account credentials (hashed password)

### 2. Child Information

- Full name
- Grade level and section
- Dietary restrictions
- Order history

### 3. Transaction Data

- Order details (items, quantities, prices)
- Payment method
- Transaction dates and times

### 4. Technical Data

- Device information
- IP address
- Browser type
- Usage logs

### 5. Optional Data

- Profile photo
- Special dietary notes
- Communication preferences

---

## How We Use Your Data

### Primary Purposes

1. **Order Processing**: Fulfill food orders
2. **Account Management**: Maintain your profile
3. **Communication**: Send order confirmations and updates
4. **Payment**: Process transactions
5. **Support**: Respond to inquiries

### Secondary Purposes

1. **Analytics**: Improve service quality
2. **Marketing**: Promotional offers (with consent)
3. **Compliance**: Legal obligations

---

## Legal Basis (Data Privacy Act)

We process your data based on:

1. **Consent**: You explicitly agree to data collection
2. **Contract**: Necessary for service delivery
3. **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with laws
4. **Legitimate Interest**: Improve services

---

## Data Sharing

### We DO share with

- **Payment Processors**: GCash, PayMongo (for transactions)
- **SMS Provider**: Twilio/Semaphore (for notifications)
- **Cloud Services**: Supabase (hosting), Vercel (frontend)

### We DO NOT share with

- Advertisers
- Third-party marketers
- Government agencies (unless legally required)

### School Access

School administrators and canteen staff can view:

- Order details
- Inventory data
- Aggregate reports

They CANNOT view:

- Parent payment information
- Personal contact details (without consent)

---

## Your Rights (Data Privacy Act)

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View your personal data
2. **Correction**: Update inaccurate data
3. **Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Portability**: Export your data
5. **Object**: Opt-out of marketing
6. **Restriction**: Limit processing

### Exercising Your Rights

Email: <privacy@yourschool.edu.ph>  
Response Time: Within 15 days

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
| --------- | ---------------- |
| Account info | Until account deletion |
| Order history | 2 years |
| Transaction records | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Logs | 90 days |
| Anonymous analytics | Indefinitely |

---

## Data Security

### Technical Measures

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens with 1-hour expiry
- **Database**: Row Level Security (RLS)
- **Backups**: Daily encrypted backups

### Organizational Measures

- Staff training on data protection
- Access controls (least privilege)
- Regular security audits

---

## Children's Data

We collect data about minors (students) but:

- Parents provide all information
- Students do not have accounts
- Data used only for order fulfillment
- Extra protection for sensitive dietary info
